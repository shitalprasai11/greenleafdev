Our Agile Process for Website Design and Development Services

We're not just creating websites for your business but adding value to your brand and helping you reach your targeted goal.

1.

Initiation

  • We start by trying to understand your objectives, business requirements, and expectations to understand your project better.
  • Our expert web designers and developers suggest you the best choice of tech selection for your project.
  • The next step is to sign an NDA (Non-disclosure Agreement) to secure your web design idea.
  • Finally, we design a sitemap and wireframe of the website which gives a basic idea of how the website will look.
2.

Iterative UX/UI Design

  • We conduct a comprehensive website development workshop to analyze all core project requirements, user behavior, and ways to make your brand a hero on the website.
  • From there, our experienced UI/UX team will create website pages based on the prototype and data collected.
  • You will get involved and updated continuously throughout the process.
3.

Website Design and Development

  • The website starts to take shape as we begin the actual website design and development.
  • The world project will be divided into numerous sprints.
  • Our website design, and development team with QAs and Scrum masters work in each sprint together.
  • The QA team tests the website from start to finish until the website is issue-free.
4.

Maintenance + Optimization

  • You'll get a regular CMS and plugin update.
  • We provide ongoing web server maintenance and repair services.
  • We will help you build a customer service strategy and refine your website services based on facts and KPIs.
